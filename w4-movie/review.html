REVIEW

Victorino Gomes August 10, 2024


Creed is a complex drama. Even though Creed is a boxing movie, it is more about the story of a boy becoming a man, but also learning how to build his own path and legacy as a man. The movie follows Adonis Creed, son of Apollo Creed build his own boxing legacy by separating himself from his fathers legacy. The story building and pacing is not only amazing but consistent through the entire movie.


My favorite part of the movie is when Adonis loses in the beginning of the movie. It helps establish how confident and cocky Adonis truly is despite his extreme skill. I liked the fact Adonis wasn't portrayed as an invinsible force.


Trivia

  • Did you know that Tony Bellow ("Pretty" Ricky Conlan) is a real boxer?
  • Michael B. Jordan actually took a real punch for a lot of the cinematic knockdown scenes.
  • Creed is the 7th film in the Rocky franchise.
